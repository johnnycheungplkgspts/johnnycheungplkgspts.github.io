    學校歷史

    保良局張凝文小學於一九四六年創立，為保良局首間開辦的學校，原名為保良局學校。初期以敎育保良局院舍的兒童為主，後來已將服務範圍擴展至其他適齡兒童。

    辦學理念以「愛、敬、勤、誠」為屬校校訓，為學生提供充實知識、訓練技能及鍛鍊體格的機會.

Supportive measures for Non-Chinese Speaking (NCS) students

Our school fully utilized the 1.5 million dollars of additional government funding received for supporting NCS students with their Chinese learning. For example, we employ extra Teachers and Teaching Assistants, going into classes to help NCS students to learn in the formal lessons. We arrange three more additional teachers to teach P.1 & P.2 and two more additional teachers to teach P.3 & P.4 Chinese Language. We have a Teacher-Assistant to teach collaboratively with the subject teacher in some of the classes, in order to help our junior class students to learn better through small group teaching as well as catering for individual differences.

Enhancement Programme

After School, there is extended learning class, called "the Chinese Reading and Writing Enhancement Programme for NCS students", enhacing Chinese languagea ability and reinforcing what they have learnt in class.

Chinese Culture Programme

For junior classes, we provide Chinese Culture Programme, enriching their knowledge about Chinese Culture, for easy adaptation to the Chinese society. For examples, they will learn about some CHinese historical great persons and traditional virtues of China.

May 2021

Cultural Harmoney Activity

Each year, we organize Cultural Harmoney Activity-Celebration of Chinese Festivals, developing NCS students' potential and helping them integrate into the social mainstream of the local society.

Chinese Study Team

We have setup a "Chinese Study Team" among the peers. Chinese students from senior classes will help P.1 NCSA students to read aloud the CHinese text or do revision for Chinese dictation.

School based curriculum

With professional guidance, our school has develop a school based curriculum for the three main subjects of Chinese Language, English Language and Mathematics, aiming at developing student's reading, writing and calculating skills with appropriate teaching straategies to meet students' need. With regards to NCS students, we adopt the strategies of learning step by step, with visual aids and prctical taks, etc.

With reference to "learning Chinese language as a second language recommended by Education Bureau", we help our NCS students to set up a learning goal and encourage them to strive and reach it step b step, enhancing the effecetiveness of their learning.
